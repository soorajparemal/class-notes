-ifconfig is used for obtaining information regarding current available networks.
-ping is used for connection status.
-Backquotes are simple yet powerful tool in shell.it first executes the command inside the quotes and then assign its value to a variable
eg::  n=`expr 1 '+' 2 ` :::::: the mathematical part inside the backquotes will be executed first and its value,ie 3,will be assigned to n.
